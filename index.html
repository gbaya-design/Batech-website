Welcome to Our School

Hope Integrated Center and School is a private institution founded in 2011. We offer Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary education in a safe, inclusive, and academically-driven environment.

Located opposite Distant Relatives Backpackers, just 1km from Kavenya off Mombasa-Malindi Road, our school provides a conducive learning atmosphere supported by qualified teaching staff, a lunch program, and free porridge for playgroup pupils.

We are currently enrolling learners at all levels and invite you to join our school community.
