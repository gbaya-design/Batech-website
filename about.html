Our Story

Hope Integrated Center and School was founded in 2011. It is led by Director Mr. Charlis Thoya Dzembe and Head Teacher Mr. Raymond Chai Chivatsi. Located in Seahorse, Kibarani Ward, opposite Distant Relatives Backpackers, the school serves the local community with dedication.

Key Details

  • Level: Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary
  • Student Population: Approx. 500 pupils
  • Staff: 14 teaching and 5 non-teaching
  • Programs: Lunch for all pupils, porridge for playgroup
  • Support: Co-curricular programs funded by international partners

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that empowers every learner with academic excellence, character development, and life skills for a better future.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that inspires holistic growth and equips learners to become responsible, innovative, and compassionate global citizens.

Our Motto

"Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures."
